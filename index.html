Crystal Collector

Here's how you play the game


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get"
  2. There are four crystals below.
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. Click on a gem and add a specific number of points to your total.
  4. If your total score matches the random number, you win!
  5. If your total score goes over the random number, you lose!
  6. Each gem is worth a randomly selected score.
  7. The gems are worth a different number of points each game.
